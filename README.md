# LexVision AI: Legal GenAI Assistant

An AI-powered legal assistant designed to simplify legal document understanding, contract analysis, and lawyer connection.  
Built to bridge the gap between **AI automation** and **professional legal expertise**.

---

##  Features / USPs
- **AI-Powered Contract Analysis** ‚Äì Extracts key clauses, obligations, and risks.  
- **Timeline Extractor** ‚Äì Pulls out important dates (lock-in period, expiry, notice period) and shows them in a visual timeline.  
- **Smart Query System** ‚Äì Ask natural language questions and get instant answers from your documents.  
- **Lawyer Marketplace** ‚Äì Connect to nearest available lawyers using geolocation.  
  - Filter by **best-rated** or **price/hour**.  
  - AI handles basics ‚Üí Lawyers provide expert advice when needed.  
- **Commission-Based Revenue Model** ‚Äì Scalable and sustainable growth.
- **Multi-Language Support** (e.g., Hindi & regional)

---

##  Solution Overview
- **End-to-End Legal Support** ‚Äì AI for first-level tasks, lawyers for critical guidance.  
- **User-Friendly Dashboard** ‚Äì Visual timelines, contract summaries, and easy search.  
- **Scalability** ‚Äì Expands across regions with location-based lawyer access.  
- **Seamless Experience** ‚Äì One platform for contracts + legal help.  

---

##  Process Flow
1. Upload contract / legal document.  
2. AI analyzes & extracts key clauses, risks, and timelines.  
3. User queries answered instantly via AI.  
4. If advanced help needed ‚Üí connect to nearest/best lawyer.  
5. Lawyer charges shown (price/hour).  
6. Final advice delivered through platform.
